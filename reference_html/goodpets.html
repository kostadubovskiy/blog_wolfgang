Do Frogs Make Good Pets?

Contrary to popular belief, Frogs are great pets. If you take good care of them they can love you (exactly like dogs), but unlike dogs they don't constantly annoy you

In this way they are very similar to Cats, but they don't have claws.

However, unlike Cats and Dogs, they don't require a lot of resources to survive - they only need water and some food

Not only are they easy to care for, they are also very fun animals to keep.

They can be trained to do things like perhaps bake cookies

Frogs are very popular pets because of the reasons stated above. They are really cool and can jump start a childs love for animals.

As mentioned before, Frogs are easy to keep and fun animals. They are sold in almost every pet store. Below I have created a list to help you out.

  1. Petco
    • Petco is the most well known petstores.
    • It has the largest selection of Frogs.
    • It also has lots of accessories for Frogs, if you already have one.
  2. Petsmart
    • Petsmart is another well known petstore.
    • They have very little selection, at the time of looking, they only had one type of Frog in many retail locations and in some they had none.
    • Additionally, their website is terrible, so I would only go to thier retail locations.
  3. Pet Supplies Plus
    • Pets Supplies Plus is another nationwide petstore.
    • However, they have the poorest choice out of all the other stores I checked.
    • I checked multiple retail locations and none of them had any Frogs
  4. Your Local Pet Shop
    • Your local petshop might have some Frogs, but most likely they will not have anything Frog related

If you decide to make a Frog your pet, you will be thrilled with the experience and definitly not disappointed
